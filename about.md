Hang Zhang is a PhD student studying computer vision in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Rutgers University. His advisor is  Prof. Kristin Dana. Before coming to Rutgers, he received his bachelor degree from  Southeast University (Nanjing, China) in 2013,  where he was working under the supervision of Prof. Junyang Li. His research interest is fast visual recognition and retrieval for materials.
